About Me

Welcome to my RAD corner of the web! 🚀

I'm Shireen, your host for this nostalgic trip down memory lane. This site is a flashback to the days of dial-up modems, floppy disks, and when every webpage had a cool spinning GIF or two (yes, they’re here too!).

🤓 A Few Bytes About Me:

🕹️ I'm a coder by day and retro gamer by night. Pac-Man, Tetris, Space Invaders, and a fan of old-school Indian games like Kho Kho and Pitthu!

💡 Obsessed with all things neon, 90s tech, and Bollywood! 🎬

🎤 Secretly dreaming of headlining a karaoke night with 90s Bollywood hits.

🌟 Why This Site?
Because the early internet was a vibe: quirky, personal, and unapologetically fun! Grab a cup of Tang, Chai or Lassi, and click your way through my little cyber galaxy. 🌌
